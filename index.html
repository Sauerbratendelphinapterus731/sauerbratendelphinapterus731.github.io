ℹ️ About this extension

This extension helps you adjust how text looks on WhatsApp Web. You can change the font style, increase or decrease the font size, and set the weight of the text to thin or bold. It works on Google Chrome, Brave, and Microsoft Edge. The tool helps you read messages with more comfort.

We built this extension with privacy in mind. It does not track your activity or collect your personal data. It uses the Manifest V3 framework to ensure high performance and safety within your browser.

🚀 Installation Guide

Follow these steps to add the extension to your browser:

  1. Visit the releases page to download the current version.
  2. Click the ZIP file to download it to your computer.
  3. Extract the contents of the ZIP folder to a known location, such as your Downloads folder.
  4. Open your web browser.
  5. Type chrome://extensions in the address bar and press Enter.
  6. Activate "Developer mode" by clicking the switch in the top right corner.
  7. Click the "Load unpacked" button.
  8. Select the folder you extracted in step 3.

⚙️ How to use

Once you install the extension, follow these steps to change your WhatsApp Web settings:

  • Open WhatsApp Web in your browser.
  • Click the extension icon from your browser toolbar.
  • Select your preferred font from the list.
  • Use the slider to set the font size.
  • Choose the weight that suits your reading style.
  • Refreshes occur automatically to show your changes.

📋 System Requirements

This extension runs on most modern desktop operating systems that support Chromium-based browsers. Ensure you have the latest version of Chrome, Brave, or Edge installed on Windows to maintain full compatibility and security.
